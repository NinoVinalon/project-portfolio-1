Kings of Europe, which is the biggest and most prominent Filipino sporting event in Europe, is scheduled to take place in Dublin, Ireland. The games will be played in four groups, and the top two teams from each group will advance to the quarterfinals. The quarterfinals will be a best-of-three series, and the winners of the quarterfinals will become the Final Four contenders.



Competition Format

The Kings of Europe Basketball League 2024 will involve 13 teams from 11 European nations consisting of Filipino athletes who will compete in the 2-day league. Games will be played on June 22 - 23 at the National Basketball Arena. Games schedules and grouping will be posted at the venue during the event.
